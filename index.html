Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about deposits, catering policies, or cancellations? We have answers.

How do I book a venue?

Search using our availability checker, click "View Details" on your preferred venue, fill in your dates/guest numbers, customize your add-ons, and click "Book Venue Now". You'll get an immediate booking confirmation code.

Can I customize decorations?

Absolutely! Our decoration panel lets you choose color palettes, stage types, and flower selections. Custom layouts can also be uploaded for our visual design partners to construct at the hall site.

How is catering charged?

Catering is calculated per guest plate. Select your menu items (e.g. Biryani, Paneer, Ice Cream), slide the guest count bar, and your total price automatically updates based on plate prices.

Can I cancel bookings?

Full refunds are issued for bookings cancelled up to 14 days before the event date. Cancelations between 7 to 13 days are eligible for a 50% refund, while cancellations under 7 days are non-refundable.

Are halls verified?

Yes. Every venue listed on EventNest undergoes a 5-step verification process checking local zoning safety licenses, electric limits, parking limits, restroom hygene, and review validity.
